Biography

I am currently pursuing the Phd degree at the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences (AMSS) and University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS). My supervisor is Dr. Xiaohong Jia. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree of mathematics and applied mathematics from Henan University of Science and Technology in 2016. I attained the national scholarship for three successive years, and I was the outstanding graduate of Henan province. I was honored the grand Phd scholarship of Saudi Arabian in 2020.

My research interest includes image processing, machine learning, and optimization.
