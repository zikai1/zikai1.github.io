Biography

I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at the Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence (BAAI) and the National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition of the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). Before that I attined the Phd degree at the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences (AMSS) and University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), under the supervision of Dr. Xiaohong Jia. I was honored the grand Phd scholarship of Saudi Arabian in 2020, and the excellent student prize of president fellowships, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2021.

My research interest includes statistical machine learning, robust estimation and Gaussian process. My current project is registration of 2D and 3D point sets.
